Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prism Gate Puzzle handles information when you use the game.

Information We Store Locally

The game stores gameplay progress, unlocked levels, best moves, star ratings, and music or sound preferences on your device. This information is used only to provide game features and is not sent to us directly.

Advertising and Third-Party SDKs

The app may use third-party advertising SDKs to request, load, and display ads. These partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, interaction data, and diagnostics to provide ads, measure performance, prevent fraud, and comply with legal requirements.

App Tracking Transparency

Where required by iOS, the app asks for your permission before accessing the advertising identifier for tracking or personalized advertising. You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings.

Network Requests

The app may request remote advertising configuration and load ad content from advertising partners. Core puzzle gameplay is designed to work offline, but ads and related services may require a network connection.

Children's Privacy

Prism Gate Puzzle is a general audience puzzle game. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review the matter.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures appropriate for a mobile game. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be identified by a new “Last updated” date.
